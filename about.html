About Me

Hi, my name is Oscar! I am a designer and illustrator based in Brooklyn, New York, studying for a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Communication Design at CUNY New York City College of Technology.

I've always enjoyed creating art, whether it's illustration, design, photo, or video. The best part of creative work is the process, with the glory at a close second place.

When I'm not drawing, I love to read, watch movies, and practice the ukulele.
